## scFv

In scFv antibodies the genes of VH and VL are joined together with a short flexible peptide linker or with disulfide bond:

- VH-linker-VL
- VL-linker-VH
- VH-SS-VL

Brolucizumab (1, Beovu) is a scFv (VL-linker-VH) approved for neovascular (wet) age-related macular degeneration treatment.  
It is administered via an injection directly into the eye to reduce abnormal blood vessel growth and excess fluid.  
It is a small (approx. 26 kDa), humanized single-chain variable fragment (scFv) antibody fragment targeting VEGF-A.

## BiTE

Blinatumomab (brand name Blincyto) is a bispecific T-cell engager (BiTE) which is built upon a tandem scFv architecture. It is constructed by linking an anti-CD19 scFv to an anti-CD3 scFv. It has been approved for the medical treatment of Philadelphia chromosome-negative relapsed or refractory B-cell precursor acute lymphoblastic leukemia.
